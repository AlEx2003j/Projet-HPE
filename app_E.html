What do you think ?

We help you to make your own opinion

We know that the world of crypto-currencies and especially Bitcoin is a place of fantasy and myth. Our goal is to pass on some of the elements that we feel are important to understand the subject, so that you can make your own opinion.

What shape for the application ?

An application that is easy to use

To make our application as easy to use as possible, we were inspired by the reference in the field, the Tinder application. We consider that the use must be intuitive so as not to alter the quality of the user experience.
All it takes is a gesture to give your opinion !

How can the app help me make up my mind ?

The choices are your opinions...

When you choose an answer for a statement that is proposed to you, you are making a choice. This choice earns you points or takes them away. It is the final calculation of points that allows the application to "rank" you in a group.
