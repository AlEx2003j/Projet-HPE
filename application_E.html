Understanding the application

The principle of the application is quite simple. Indeed, once you have arrived on the Bitcoin positioning test, you have 2 choices: either to the right to approve the statement on the map, or to the left to disagree.

Once the swipe action is completed, the answer will either earn you points or take them away. The basic value for the storyteller is 0. All questions give or take away the same number of points.

It is interesting to note that the questions are not repeated. The purpose is not to look for inconsistencies in your answers, but to assess your level of knowledge and to form your own opinion on the question: Bitcoin - speculative currency or currency of the future?

Once you've answered all the questions, you'll have a certain number of points that will fit into a score range that will allow an algorithm to "rank" you and give you a result. This ranking is not totally representative of your knowledge and opinions as the subject is very broad and difficult to focus on as a whole.
